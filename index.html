ABOUT NICOLAS



Nicolas Russo is an independent artist as well as an audio and sofware engineer. He currently attends the University of Miami in Florida, pursuing a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Music Engineering Technology with a specialization in Computer Engineering. In his time at the university, Nicolas has recorded several groups in multiple studios, including the esteemed Weeks Recording Studio.


His musical background in piano, ectronic instrumental performance, and both contemporary and jazz drumset has provided him with a wide range of interests and talents. He also creates heavy electronic dance music under the name Omnicorum, where he has released over 46 songs on multiple record labels.


Nicolas also has a large passion for computer science, engineering, and programming. In addition to a multitude of various independent projects he has completed, Nicolas also worked as a Teacher's Assistant for the Intro to Programming class at the University of Miami. There, he taught a weekly lab section, where he kept students on task, answered questions, and provided guidance where needed. He also graded and provided feedback to students' lab work, as well as facilitating one-on-one and group tutoring sessions.
